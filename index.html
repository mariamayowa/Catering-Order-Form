Welcome to Taste of Lagos – a mobile Essex based Nigerian catering company, serving London, Essex and Kent. A high quality, delicious and authentic UK-based Nigerian catering company.

Taste of Lagos’ most popular food item is its authentic Nigerian Shawarma, which tastes like it is straight from Lagos.
